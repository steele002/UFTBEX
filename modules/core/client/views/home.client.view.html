Textbooks for every student

Textbooks at UF is an application which provides an enriching marketplace experience for buying and selling used textbooks among students at the University of Florida.

Personalize Textbook Listings

Use the UF Texbook Adoption data to provide a detailed listing.

Search All Types of Textbooks

Narrow down textbooks by their title, author, class, professor, or price.

Connect With Other Students

Message sellers directly on a listing to communicate your interest.
