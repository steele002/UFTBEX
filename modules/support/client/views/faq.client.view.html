Where can I view/edit my listings?

You can view your listings by clicking on the top right dropdown menu and selecting My Profile. Your listings will show up on the right side of the screen. Clicking the listings name lets you view the project, clicking the button lets you edit the project. (This button doesn't do anything, it is just a sample of what you will see)

Where can I edit my profile settings?

You can view your profile settings by clicking on the top right dropdown menu and selecting Edit Profile.
